#  Multi-Zone Open Rack
Arbin Instruments offers a Multi-Zone Open Rack, or MZOR. This is a battery holder system that features four compartments for holding battery trays. The rack provides an open environment with no isolation.

##  Introduction to the Arbin Multi-Zone Open Rack
![ARBIN-Introduction to the Arbin Multi-Zone Open Rack](https://www.arbin.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/ARBIN-Introduction-to-the-Arbin-Multi-Zone-Open-Rack.png)
Arbin Instruments offers a Multi-Zone Open Rack, or MZOR. This is a battery holder system that features four compartments for holding battery trays. The rack provides an open environment with no isolation.
A variety of trays are offered which allow testing of many different battery formats. The trays are sold separately.
The electrical connections to the trays are made through connectors at the back of the compartment so the equipment operator does not have to interact with the cabling, increasing safety and decreasing risk of operator error.
Electrical connections are provided to accommodate either second voltage input cables or PT100 Resistance Temperature Detectors (RTDs) for measurement of the temperature of the cells under test. The PT100 RTDs and cables are sold separately.
The MZOR systems are stackable using racks offered by Arbin Instruments. The racks are available in configurations to hold 4, 6, 8 or 12 MZOR systems each. The racks are sold separately.
