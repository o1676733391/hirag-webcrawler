#  High-Precision Battery Tester
Arbin's High-Precision Battery Tester (HPS) is equipped with state-of-the-art technology, ensuring unmatched accuracy and precision.

##  Highlights
  * 6 Current Ranges per Channel​
  * 10ppm Measurement Precision​
  * 50ppm Measurement Accuracy​
  * High resolution 24bit DAC structure​
  * High resolution 24bit ADC structure​
  * Built-in additional reference electrodes​

  * Built-in thermistor PT100 input per channel​
  * Built-in EIS measurement per Channel​
  * Built-in DCIM Measurement feature​
  * Built-in Self Discharge Current Measurement feature​
  * Ideal for coulombic efficiency test​
  * Integrate Gamry EIS up to 100kHz​

####  High confidence Coulumbic Efficiency measurement
The HPT is a low current battery tester with high precision measurement capabilities to detect minute signatures of battery degradation trends early in the testing life cycle. This greatly reduces the battery development cycle while also providing high confidence data.
![ARBIN-LBTS21324-Battery-Testing-Analysis-better](https://www.arbin.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/ARBIN-LBTS21324-Battery-Testing-Analysis-better.png)
![ARBIN-LBTS21324-Battery-Testing-Analysis-current](https://www.arbin.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/ARBIN-LBTS21324-Battery-Testing-Analysis-current.png)
####  [ Fast self-discharge current measurement(SDCM) ](https://www.arbin.com/innovation/self-discharge-current-measurement/ "Fast self-discharge current measurement\(SDCM\)")
Arbin's patent-pending SDCM feature greatly reduce self-discharge current measurement time to 0.5~2 days. It applies multiple low currents to the cell and carefully measures cell's voltage change rate accordingly, thus derive the self-discharge current.
