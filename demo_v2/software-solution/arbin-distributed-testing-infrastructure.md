An AIBin file contains the firmware's hex as well as hardware-firmware-software compatibility details to prevent version-matching issues.

##  Advantages
### Flexible Control
Our Decentralized Testing Infrastructure is engineered for enterprises that require a scalable, flexible, and collaborative approach to battery testing.
### Simplified Network Topology
This networked solution allows you to manage testing operations across multiple locations with ease, ensuring consistent and high-quality results.
### No dependency on central servers
Each testing station is independent from central control, with central coordination across your entire testing lab. Given a great flexibility for each battery testers with specific testing setup without depend upon IT or infrastructure team.
### Centralized Control Distributed Stations
Manage multiple hardware units across different locations from a centralized host. Our infrastructure supports both desktop applications and website services, enabling seamless control and coordination across your entire operation.
### Collaborative Testing Environment
Enable teams in various locations to work together effectively. With centralized control and real-time data streaming via Kafka, your team can monitor and adjust tests collaboratively, ensuring consistency and quality across all testing sites.
### Flexible Hardware Control
Each hardware unit can be controlled by one or multiple computers, with C#, Python, and WebAPI support for advanced, customized operations. This flexibility allows you to adapt your infrastructure as your testing needs evolve.
