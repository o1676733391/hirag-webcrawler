#  Arbin to Exhibit and Present at Batteries Event Lyon 2025 — Join Us in November
October 1, 2025
We’re excited to share that **Arbin Instruments** will be attending [**Batteries Event 2025**](https://batteries-event.com/) in Lyon, France from **November 4 to 6, 2025**.
Batteries Event is one of Europe’s premier gatherings for the full battery value chain — from raw materials and cell design to manufacturing, testing, second-life, and recycling. Batteries Event This year marks the **27th edition** of the conference, and the format has been refreshed to bring together key stakeholders, decision makers, and innovators in one forum.
![batteries event 2025 in lyon france 2](https://www.arbin.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/batteries-event-2025-in-lyon-france-2.png)
### Why We’re Attending
At Arbin, we’re passionate about powering advancements in battery technology — from high-precision testing to long-term reliability studies. Participating in Batteries Event will give us a platform to:
  * Showcase our latest test and measurement solutions tailored for next-generation battery systems
  * Engage directly with battery manufacturers, researchers, OEMs, and recyclers
  * Gather feedback on real-world challenges from the field
  * Stay updated of trends and breakthroughs across materials, cell architectures, and lifecycle strategies

### What to Expect at the Event
  * **Exhibition Booth & Demonstrations**: Drop by to see live demos, and explore how Arbin’s systems can integrate into your R&D or production workflow.
  * **Networking & Collaboration**: We’re eager to connect with prospective partners in both industrial and academic sectors
  * **Panel Sessions & Talks**: While the full 2025 program is still being published, Batteries Event traditionally hosts keynote sessions, technical presentations, and panel debates focusing on battery innovation, regulation, sustainability, and commercialization.

### Mark Your Calendars & Connect With Us
  * **Dates** : November 4 – 6, 2025
  * **Venue** : Booth A29, Lyon Convention Centre, Lyon, France

We’d love to see you there! If you plan to attend, drop us a line — we can arrange to meet, demo our latest hardware, or discuss test strategies over coffee.
Stay tuned: in the weeks ahead, we’ll share what exactly we’ll bring to the show, when we will be presenting, and how to find our booth on the floor plan.
Let the countdown begin — we look forward to seeing you in Lyon.
— The Arbin Instruments Team
