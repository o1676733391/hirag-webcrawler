Experience unparalleled performance and reliability in battery formation with our cutting-edge, patented technology. # Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Channels | 336 channels for 18650/21700 cell or 128 channels for 46xx cell, custom design available for prismatic and pouch cell. |
| Patented Technology | Series Parallel Technology (SPT) structure. |
| Complete Process | Covers complete battery formation process. |
| Self-Discharge Current Measurement | Equipped with Self-Discharge Current Measurement (SDCM) feature. |
| Early Detection | Monitoring each cell's self-discharge current by conducting SDM step at different formation stages, thus detects cell failure at early stage, hours/days before cell's thermal runaway. |
