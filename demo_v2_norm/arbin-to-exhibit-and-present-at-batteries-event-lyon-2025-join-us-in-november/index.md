# Overview

We’re excited to share that **Arbin Instruments** will be attending **Batteries Event 2025** in Lyon, France from **November 4 to 6, 2025**. Batteries Event is one of Europe’s premier gatherings for the full battery value chain — from raw materials and cell design to manufacturing, testing, second-life, and recycling. This year marks the **27th edition** of the conference, and the format has been refreshed to bring together key stakeholders, decision makers, and innovators in one forum.

# Detailed Explanation

At Arbin, we’re passionate about powering advancements in battery technology — from high-precision testing to long-term reliability studies. Participating in Batteries Event will give us a platform to:

- Showcase our latest test and measurement solutions tailored for next-generation battery systems.
- Engage directly with battery manufacturers, researchers, OEMs, and recyclers.
- Gather feedback on real-world challenges from the field.
- Stay updated on trends and breakthroughs across materials, cell architectures, and lifecycle strategies.

## What to Expect at the Event

- **Exhibition Booth & Demonstrations**: Drop by to see live demos, and explore how Arbin’s systems can integrate into your R&D or production workflow.
- **Networking & Collaboration**: We’re eager to connect with prospective partners in both industrial and academic sectors.
- **Panel Sessions & Talks**: While the full 2025 program is still being published, Batteries Event traditionally hosts keynote sessions, technical presentations, and panel debates focusing on battery innovation, regulation, sustainability, and commercialization.
