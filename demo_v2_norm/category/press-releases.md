# Detailed Explanation

## Turnkey Battery Testing

Arbin’s Turnkey Battery Testing is an all-in-one cell testing solution that includes integrated test channels, temperature chambers, and cell holders. It features a precision measurement capability of 100ppm with industry-leading 24-bit resolution across four current ranges per test channel. The system is a fully integrated solution that arrives with channel and temperature cables interconnected from channel to MZTC. Dynamic temperature setpoints are managed independently per isolated thermal chamber.

## Honeywell and Arbin Instruments Collaboration

Honeywell has enhanced its Battery Manufacturing Excellence Platform (MXP) for lithium-ion battery manufacturers and gigafactories through a collaboration with Arbin Instruments. This partnership aims to integrate an advanced autonomous formation system to improve manufacturing processes.

## Ambri's Development Lab Expansion

Ambri has selected Arbin Instruments to provide high current battery testing systems for the expansion of its next-generation development lab. These systems will support the development and commercialization of Ambri’s revolutionary Liquid Metal™ batteries, which are designed for long-duration stationary energy storage applications in electric grids and datacenters.
