# Overview

Battery holder for high capacity cells up to 200A with adjustable length and diameter for cylindrical cells with opposing terminals. Allows 4-point connection with independent current and voltage measurement. This holder is ideal for testing high capacity 18650 and 2170 cells as well as newer 4680 and 48120 sizes. Arbin PN: 470546.
