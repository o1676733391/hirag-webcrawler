# Detailed Explanation

The integration of Arbin Instruments with Gamry potentiostats allows for high-frequency electrochemical impedance spectroscopy (EIS) testing. This capability is essential for evaluating battery performance and health. The article discusses the challenges faced when integrating these systems and presents a method to overcome the limitations imposed by standard specifications.
