# Overview

Arbin’s High Current Cylindrical Battery Holder is designed for high energy density and high capacity cells, specifically the new 2170 format cell. It accommodates currents ranging from 5A up to 200A and allows for quick setup and connection to Arbin’s battery test equipment or other testing assemblies.
