# Overview

Arbin's SBTR-OTO battery tray features advanced thermal management with integrated thermoelectric cooling (TEC) modules to provide precise, independent temperature control for each cell. Designed for high-performance testing environments, each cell is seated within a machined aluminum housing that ensures direct thermal contact with the TEC module. The opposite side of the TEC is exposed to ambient conditions窶杯ypically an environmental chamber窶蚤llowing for passive or chamber-assisted heat rejection.
