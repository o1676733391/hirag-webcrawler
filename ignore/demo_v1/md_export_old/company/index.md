##  30 years anniversary
Innovation through research and development has been a core value for Arbin since the beginning.
We were the first company to offer a multi-channeled **potentiostatic / galvanostatic device** , multiple current ranges, the ability to simulate telecom pulses, the first to offer true high-precision testing capability for high currents **(up to 200A)** and now the first offer thermal runaway prevention for battery manufacturing using patented self-discharge monitoring!
