#  Overview about what Arbin products to choose – Voltage and Current Range
October 8, 2025
#### **Arbin’s Battery Testing Systems**
Arbin’s main products are advanced **battery testing systems** that accurately simulate real-world charge and discharge cycles. These systems help researchers, engineers, and manufacturers evaluate battery performance, safety, and lifespan under a wide range of conditions.
#### **Key Specifications**
Two of the most important specifications of a testing system are its **maximum channel voltage** and **maximum channel current**. Together, these determine the limits at which the system can safely charge and discharge a battery – making them essential factors when selecting the right solution for your application.
#### **What This Article Covers**
This article provides an overview of the **voltage ranges** and **current ranges** available across Arbin’s main product lines. In a follow-up article, we will explore Arbin’s **auxiliary products** , which further expand testing capabilities.
![arbin product overview chart](https://www.arbin.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/arbin-product-overview-chart.png)
Arbin Product Overview Graph above illustrates the **current** and **voltage ranges** of Arbin’s main products. However, a bit of elaboration will help clarify the details:
#### **LBTS Series**
**LBTS** stands for **Laboratory Battery Tester** , and is actually the next generation of the original **LBT** series.
This product line is divided into two categories: **LBTS-Cell** and **LBTS-Module**.
### **LBTS-Cell**
  * **Max Voltage Range:** 5V – 10V
  * **5V Range:**

– Max Current: **20A per channel**
– For high-channel-count systems, the **recommended current** is 10A per channel.
  * **10V Range:**

– Max Current: **300A per channel**
### **LBTS-Module**
  * **Max Voltage Range:** 20V – 150V
  * **Max Current Range:** 80A

#### **RBT4 Series**
### **RBT4-Cell**
  * **Max Current Range:** 100A – 400A per channel (parallel up to 1600A)
  * **Max Voltage:** 20V

### **RBT4-Module**
  * **Current Range:** 75A – 300A per channel (parallel up to 1200A)
  * **Max Voltage Range:** 30V – 200V

###  **RBT3** offers the highest current and voltage ranges of all Arbin products.
  * **Max Current Range:** 150A – 400A per channel (parallel to achieve thousands of amps)
  * **Max Voltage Range:** 300V – 1000V

You can see that RBT4 maximum voltage is only up to 200V. Therefore, when you want to test something higher than 200V but still less than 300V, we will need to introduce RBT3 300V, this will also cover the voltage range that is bigger than 200V but still smaller than 300V
### **Parallel Capability:**
One of Arbin’s advantages is the ability to parallel channels to achieve higher current.
For example, you can combine four 400A channels to achieve 1600A. **Higher voltage may also be achieved by hardware serializing multiple** **channels** (RBT series only).
