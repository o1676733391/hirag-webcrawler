#  Arbin Instruments at The Battery Show Asia 2026
January 12, 2026
We are excited to share that **Arbin Instruments** will be participating in **The Battery Show Asia 2026** , held from **March 10–12, 2026** at **AsiaWorld-Expo in Hong Kong** — one of the most influential gatherings in the global battery and energy storage industry.
This premier **three-day business-to-business exhibition and conference** brings together **over 20,000 industry professionals** , including policymakers, engineers, manufacturers, innovators, and thought leaders from across the energy, electric vehicle, and storage ecosystems.
The event provides an outstanding platform to:
  * Showcase cutting-edge battery technologies, advanced energy storage systems, and testing solutions
  * Discover breakthroughs in battery materials, manufacturing, and sustainability
  * Connect with global partners, OEMs, and industry experts
  * Explore opportunities in emerging markets across Asia and beyond

Arbin Instruments looks forward to engaging with partners and customers, sharing insights on the latest advances in battery testing, and helping drive innovation across the battery value chain.
Stay tuned for updates from the show and let’s power the future of energy together!
**For more information, please visit:** <https://www.thebatteryshow.asia/>
