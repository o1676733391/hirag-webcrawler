##  [ Battery Testing Analysis ](https://www.arbin.com/software-solution/test-analysis.html "Battery Testing Analysis")
####  Better battery comparison test with parallelled cells
PDBT performs battery comparison test by providing real battery parallel circuit and applying a bulk current to them, while the current distributed among cells naturally, all cells status tends to be more balanced. In addition to regular charge/discharge data, this structure provides additional current discrepancy value among cells to reveal cell's difference more intuitively.

####  Current change across parallel connected batteries
This plot demonstrates the gradual balancing of currents among parallel-connected batteries during a bulk discharge, highlighting how the current distribution becomes more uniform over time. It provides insights into the behavior of battery packs within complex structures.
