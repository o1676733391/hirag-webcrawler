# Overview

As a leader in battery testing, manufacturing, and research, we drive innovation that sets industry standards. For over 30 years, our advanced technologies have transformed battery research and testing, empowering researchers and manufacturers to achieve superior performance and shape the future of energy storage.

# Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| DCIM    | Cutting-edge technology and an innovative alternative to the traditional ACIM technique for measuring battery impedance, uses Galvanostatic and AI Prediction Modeling Methods provide distinct advantages without additional equipment is required, shorter test times, and it maintains accuracy comparable to ACIM. |
| SDCM    | Essential for evaluating a battery's state of health, predicting its performance, and estimating its lifespan. This method has been revolutionized to be significantly faster and more accurate while lowering equipment requirements. |
| SPT     | Technology arranges cells with parallel and serial connection, each cell has dedicated monitoring circuit (relay and sample resistor) with it. This structure allows bulk inlet current to naturally distribute into each cell based on its real capacity and allows minor current flow among paralleled cells for self-balancing. |
