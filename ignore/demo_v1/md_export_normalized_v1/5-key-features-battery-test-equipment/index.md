# Overview

Test equipment should facilitate rather than frustrate the testing process. Good equipment allows researchers to pick up the smallest of changes during the experiment. Durable and reliable equipment have a mixture of qualities that work together for an accurate and efficient testing process.

# Key Features

| Feature               | Description                                                                 |
|----------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| High resolution       | 24-bit resolution allows for higher clarity in data compared to the industry standard of 16-bit. |
| High precision        | Less noise/fluctuation in measurements ensures consistency and repeatability. |
| Accuracy              | Closeness of the average sample to its true value, best evaluated with high resolution and precision. |
| Minimal temperature fluctuations | Equipment minimizes the impact of temperature on measurements through design and materials. |
| Robust materials      | Equipment made with quality materials that are resistant to corrosion and temperature. |
