# Overview

Battery recycling is crucial in addressing battery waste management. Proper recycling ensures that cells are taken care of properly, protecting people and the environment from the adverse effects of ill-disposed batteries.

# Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Lead-acid battery recycling | Mature process with high recycling rates for lead and plastic. |
| Lithium-ion battery recycling | Two processes: pyrometallurgical and hydrometallurgical. |
| Recovery rates | Up to 95-98% for certain metals using advanced methods. |
