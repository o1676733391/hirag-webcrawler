# Overview

Structural batteries are designed to serve as both energy storage and load-bearing components, potentially reducing the overall weight of electric vehicles and other applications.

# Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Massless batteries | Batteries that do not add extra mass to the device beyond necessary structural elements. |
| Carbon fiber | A common material used for structural batteries due to its strength and high ion-carrying capacity. |
| Energy density | The latest version of a massless battery has an energy density of 24 wh/kg, which is 20% of a lithium-ion battery's capacity. |
