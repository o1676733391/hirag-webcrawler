# Overview

Self-healing batteries have been a notable area of battery research and development, particularly concerning dendrites and their impact on performance. Initial research has opened the door to the possibility of safer and longer-lasting batteries.

# Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Self-healing capability | Batteries that can repair internal damage to enhance longevity and safety. |
| Solid-polymer electrolyte | A type of battery that can self-heal after damage. |
| Potassium-based battery | A battery that self-heals by reversing dendrite growth. |
