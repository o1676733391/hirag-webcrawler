# Overview

Flying cars, specifically eVTOLs (Electrical Vertical Take-Off & Landing), are being developed as alternatives to conventional cars, aiming to alleviate road traffic and provide a more environmentally friendly travel option. However, battery technology still needs significant improvements for these vehicles to become viable.

# Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| eVTOLs  | Electrical Vertical Take-Off & Landing vehicles, also known as passenger drones. |
| Hybrid vehicles | Vehicles that can operate on both roads and in the air. |
| Energy efficiency | eVTOLs aim to improve energy use compared to conventional vehicles. |
