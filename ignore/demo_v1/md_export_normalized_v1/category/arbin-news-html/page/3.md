# Overview

Batteries are a critical component of many products, and energy storage plays a very active role in our lives even outside of the research/industry setting. Therefore, selecting the right battery test equipment is an important decision for companies and the individual researchers who are responsible for producing results.

# Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Software usability | Software interface can be a main differentiating point besides hardware performance. |
| Accuracy | Represents the trueness of test equipment measurement; the closeness of average sample to its true value. |
| Robustness | Quality of materials and construction impact the longevity and calibration of test equipment. |
| Temperature | Variance affects measurement; knowing how it impacts results is crucial. |
| Precision | Determines the level of noise/fluctuation in measurements. |
| Resolution | Indicates the smallest change in measurement detectable by the instrument. |
