# Overview

Arbin Instruments offers a Multi-Zone Open Rack, or MZOR. This is a battery holder system that features four compartments for holding battery trays. The rack provides an open environment with no isolation.

# Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Compartments | 4 for holding battery trays |
| Stackable | Yes, using racks offered by Arbin Instruments |
| Safety | Electrical connections made through connectors at the back of the compartment |

# Notes

* A variety of trays are offered which allow testing of many different battery formats. The trays are sold separately.
* Electrical connections to the trays are made through connectors at the back of the compartment so the equipment operator does not have to interact with the cabling, increasing safety and decreasing risk of operator error.
* PT100 RTDs and cables are sold separately.
