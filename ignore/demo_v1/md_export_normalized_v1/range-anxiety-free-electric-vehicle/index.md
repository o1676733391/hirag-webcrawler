# Overview

Lucid Motors has announced a new all-electric vehicle, the Lucid Air sedan, with a range of 517 miles on a single charge, addressing range anxiety and pushing for EV adoption.

# Key Features

| Feature                  | Description                                      |
|-------------------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| Long range              | 517 miles on a single charge                     |
| Rapid charging           | 900-volt charger with a peak charging rate of over 300 kW |
| Bi-directional charging  | Allows the car to power the home and sell energy back to the grid |
