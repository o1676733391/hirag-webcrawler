# Overview

The Nickel-Metal Hydride (NiMH) battery is a type of rechargeable battery that uses nickel oxide hydroxide (NiOOH) as the positive electrode and a hydrogen-absorbing alloy as the negative electrode. NiMH batteries offer higher capacity, lower environmental impact, and are widely used in modern electronic devices and vehicles.
