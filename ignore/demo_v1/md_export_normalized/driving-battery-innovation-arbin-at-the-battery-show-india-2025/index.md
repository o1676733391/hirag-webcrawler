# Overview

We are excited to share that Arbin will be participating in The Battery Show India 2025, taking place from October 30 to November 1 at the India Expo Mart & Centre in Hall 7, Booth B352, Greater Noida. As a global leader in high-precision battery testing technology, Arbin provides advanced solutions for battery cells, modules, and energy storage systems.
