# Detailed Explanation

## Three Benefits of Central Battery Test System Control
Centralized battery testing management provides several advantages for battery testing labs. Simplifying equipment and test management, centralized control supports labs and researchers to conduct more productive and efficient tests.

## Managing Your Arbin Systems with Central Monitor and Control Solution (CMCS)
Arbin’s CMCS simplifies battery test equipment management as you oversee tests and test status from multiple cyclers on a connected PC. It also allows for user access levels, notifications of tester status, and integration with third-party data analysis tools.

## Arbin Instruments Establishes Office in Pune, India
Arbin Instruments, manufacturer of high-precision battery test equipment, is pleased to announce the opening of its India office in Pune.

## 5 Reasons Your Lab Needs a Battery Rack Connection System
A well-planned battery rack connection system allows researchers to set up and organize their testing equipment to optimize the safety and efficiency of the lab. We highlight five key ways battery racks are beneficial.

## How Arbin’s Systems Support Real Life Battery Simulations
Real-life simulations in battery testing provide a more accurate estimate of actual battery performance. Learn how the features of Arbin’s RBT series support dynamic test profiles to provide researchers with more reliable battery performance results.

## 30th Anniversary- Celebrating the Past and Looking Towards the Future
This year will mark a big milestone for Arbin Instruments, as the leader in the battery and energy storage testing space will turn 30. Learn more how Arbin can help you in your battery testing needs.

## Using a Three-Electrode Cell in Battery Testing
Arbin’s new 3E Coin Cell provides users with an affordable, easy-to-use three-electrode cell holder that allows for long-term cycling and provides consistent results between samples.

## 5 Features of Arbin’s Regenerative Battery Testing Series
Arbin’s Regenerative Battery Testing (RBT) series is designed specifically for high-power batteries used in applications such as electric vehicles, military, and stationary grid storage.

## The Benefits of Arbin Instruments’ Three-Electrode Coincell Test Solution
By leveraging Arbin’s complete three-electrode testing solution, you can get clear, accurate, and efficient results.

## 5 Key Features of Reliable and Efficient Battery Test Equipment
High-quality test equipment accelerates development by providing reliable and precise data for the research process. Learn how Arbin can help you take on your next challenge.

## Safe Testing for EV Batteries
Why researching Electric Vehicles batteries has never been safer. Advancement in battery testing equipment has made the process of battery testing safer. With more precise and accurate testing equipment, the smallest changes in a battery can be detected before it becomes a critical issue. Improved equipment also allows for a more stable and controlled testing environment.

Arbin is expanding in 2019 to increase production line testing capability. Dozens of new input power lines have already been installed in our 65,000 sqft. headquarters in Texas. There has been extreme demand for our high-power battery testing systems and we are working hard to keep up.
