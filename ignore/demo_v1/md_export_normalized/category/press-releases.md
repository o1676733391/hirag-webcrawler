# Detailed Explanation

## Turnkey Battery Testing

Arbin’s Turnkey Battery Testing is an all-in-one solution that includes integrated test channels, temperature chambers, and cell holders. It provides a measurement precision of 100 ppm with industry-leading 24-bit resolution across four current ranges per test channel. The system arrives fully integrated with channel and temperature cables interconnected from channel to MZTC. Dynamic temperature setpoints are managed independently per isolated thermal chamber.

## Honeywell and Arbin Instruments Collaboration

Honeywell has enhanced its Battery Manufacturing Excellence Platform (MXP) for lithium-ion battery manufacturers and gigafactories through a collaboration with Arbin Instruments. This partnership aims to integrate an advanced autonomous formation system to improve manufacturing processes.

## Ambri's Development Lab Expansion

Ambri has selected Arbin Instruments to provide high current battery testing systems for its pilot manufacturing expansion. These systems will support the development and commercialization of Ambri’s revolutionary Liquid Metal™ batteries, which are designed for long-duration stationary energy storage applications in electric grids and datacenters.
