# Overview

Arbin is a global leader in manufacturing test equipment for batteries and other energy storage device applications. The company invests nearly half its profits back into research and development to ensure it stays ahead of industry trends and competitors. As a privately-owned company, Arbin promotes a family/team-oriented culture with regular company-wide events and a friendly atmosphere.

# Working at Arbin

Arbin offers a great balance of work and leisure whether you enjoy the great outdoors or the great indoors. During lunch, employees frequent several walking trails around Arbin’s property when the weather is nice or make internal laps around the 65,000 sq. ft. facility. If fishing is more your style, a covered deck offers relaxation in the well-stocked pond. Another common occurrence during lunch and after work is to hear the familiar patter of ping pong balls and joyful exclamations on one of three table tennis courts.

# Ways to Help Our Team

Arbin is a thriving company in the energy storage industry and is always looking for highly qualified individuals in the following departments. Please submit your resume if you are interested in contributing to our team.

## Research and Development

From custom projects to improving existing designs, Research and Development has been one of Arbin’s primary strengths since the beginning. Arbin needs qualified Electrical and Mechanical Engineers for circuit and hardware design for its entire range of products.

## Software

Arbin has developed multiple software interfaces for its testing systems over the years and needs qualified Software Engineers to develop and maintain existing and new products, as well as to develop internal sales and support database tools.

## Customer Support

Arbin prides itself on world-class customer support. The company needs qualified engineers to troubleshoot and improve on existing designs in both software and hardware.

## Sales

Arbin offers several standard product lines, but customization is one of its biggest strengths. Therefore, Arbin hires Engineers and other technically oriented people who can communicate its products effectively and provide customers with solutions that fulfill their particular battery testing needs.
