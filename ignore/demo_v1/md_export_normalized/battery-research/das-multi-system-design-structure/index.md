# Overview

Arbin products deliver fast, accurate **Self-Discharge Current** measurement while maintaining battery electrochemical balance. Our technology allows the applied small current to be constant and unaffected by temperature changes, ensuring precision throughout the process.

# Notes

*Measurement precision is more critical for long-term battery testing and projections than control accuracy alone.*
*What affects test equipment precision includes resolution of DAC, resolution of ADC, non-linearity of calibration, short-term drift (temperature), and long-term drift (material characteristics).*
*Arbin's improvements include higher resolution, improved software algorithms, new methods of thermal management, new patented shunt design, and new method of time keeping.*
