# Notes

- Measurement precision is critical for long-term battery testing and projections.
- Important trends and electrochemical indicators may remain unnoticed due to measurement noise.
- **What Affects Test Equipment Precision**: Resolution of DAC, Resolution of ADC, Non-linearity of calibration, Short-term drift (temperature), Long-term drift (material characteristics).
- **Arbin Test Equipment Improvements**: Higher Resolution, Improved Software Algorithms, New Methods of Thermal Management, New Patented Shunt Design, New Method of Time Keeping, New Materials.
- *Dr. John Zhang emphasizes the importance of precision in battery testing equipment.*
