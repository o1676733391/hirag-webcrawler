# Overview

We are dedicated to delivering cutting-edge products and unparalleled support that enable our customers to achieve excellence in energy storage, e-mobility, advanced battery manufacturing, and materials research. Our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction ensures that we provide the tools necessary for our customers to push the boundaries of technology and create a sustainable future.

# Detailed Explanation

Innovation through research and development has been a core value for Arbin since the beginning. We were the first company to offer a multi-channeled potentiostatic/galvanostatic device, multiple current ranges, the ability to simulate telecom pulses, the first to offer true high-precision testing capability for high currents (up to 200A), and now the first to offer thermal runaway prevention for battery manufacturing using patented self-discharge monitoring.
