# Detailed Explanation

## DCIM
Cutting-edge technology and an innovative alternative to the traditional ACIM technique for measuring battery impedance, using Galvanostatic and AI Prediction Modeling Methods provides distinct advantages without requiring additional equipment, shorter test times, and maintains accuracy comparable to ACIM.

## Self Discharge Current Measurement
SDCM is essential for evaluating a battery's state of health, predicting its performance, and estimating its lifespan. We have revolutionized this method, making it significantly faster and more accurate while lowering equipment requirements.

## Serial Parallel Cell Structure
Arbin Patented (US11933860B2) SPT technology arranges cells with parallel and serial connections, with each cell having a dedicated monitoring circuit (relay and sample resistor). This structure allows bulk inlet current to naturally distribute into each cell based on its real capacity and allows minor current flow among paralleled cells for self-balancing.
