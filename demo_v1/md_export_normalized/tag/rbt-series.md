# Overview

Arbin products deliver fast, accurate **Self-Discharge Current** measurement while maintaining battery electrochemical balance. Our technology allows the applied small current to be constant and unaffected by temperature changes, ensuring precision throughout the process.

# Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Self-Discharge Current Measurement | Fast and accurate measurement while maintaining electrochemical balance. |
| DCIM | Innovative alternative to traditional ACIM technique for measuring battery impedance. |
| SDCM | Essential for evaluating a battery's state of health, predicting performance, and estimating lifespan. |
| Serial Parallel Cell Structure | Patented technology that arranges cells with parallel and serial connections for self-balancing. |

# Notes

*Measurement precision is critical for long-term battery testing and projections.*
*What affects test equipment precision includes resolution of DAC, resolution of ADC, non-linearity of calibration, short-term drift (temperature), and long-term drift (material characteristics).*
*Arbin's test equipment improvements include higher resolution, improved software algorithms, new methods of thermal management, new patented shunt design, and new method of time keeping.*
