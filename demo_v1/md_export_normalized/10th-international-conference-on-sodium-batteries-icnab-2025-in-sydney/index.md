# Overview

The 10th International Conference on Sodium Batteries (ICNaB) 2025 is hosted by the University of Technology Sydney at the Amora Hotel Jamison Sydney. This event serves as a vital platform for leading academics, emerging researchers, and industry pioneers to exchange ideas, present cutting-edge research, and foster meaningful collaboration in the field of sodium-ion battery technology.

# Detailed Explanation

As global demand grows for sustainable, cost-effective, and scalable energy storage solutions, sodium-ion batteries are emerging as a compelling alternative to lithium-based systems. The ICNaB conference will highlight breakthroughs in materials science, electrochemical performance, battery architecture, and commercialization strategies—driving us closer to a cleaner energy future.

At Arbin Instruments, we are proud to support this important initiative. With nearly 35 years of experience in high-precision battery testing systems, we remain committed to empowering innovation across the battery industry. We invite you to visit us at the conference and discover our latest technological advancements designed to support next-generation battery research and development.
