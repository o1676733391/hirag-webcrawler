# Detailed Explanation

## DCIM
Cutting-edge technology and an innovative alternative to the traditional ACIM technique for measuring battery impedance, uses Galvanostatic and AI Prediction Modeling Methods provide distinct advantages without additional equipment, shorter test times, and it maintains accuracy comparable to ACIM.

## Self Discharge Current Measurement
SDCM is essential for evaluating a battery's state of health, predicting its performance, and estimating its lifespan. We have revolutionized this method, making it significantly faster and more accurate while lowering equipment requirements.

## Serial Parallel Cell Structure
Arbin Patented (US11933860B2) SPT technology arranges cells with parallel and serial connections, each cell has a dedicated monitoring circuit (relay and sample resistor). This structure not only lets bulk inlet current naturally distribute into each cell based on its real capacity but also allows minor current flow among paralleled cells so that they can be self-balanced.
