#  Expansion in 2019
December 20, 2018
Arbin is expanding in 2019 to increase production line testing capability. Dozens of new input power lines have already been installed in our 65,000 sqft. headquarters in Texas.
There has been extreme demand for our high-power [battery testing systems](https://www.arbin.com/products/battery-test-equipment/) and we are working hard to keep up.
