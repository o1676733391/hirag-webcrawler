##  Battery Racks

**Choose Rack Size:** 5-layer, 8-layer, 16-layer, or 24-layer
**Choose Tray Type:** Cylindrical Cells, Flat/Pouch Cells, or Plain Shelf (can be used to organize other battery holders)
**Choose Cable Length:** 6ft. (~2m), 12ft. (~3.5m), or 20ft. (~6m)

**Rack Material:** Aluminum
**Space Between Layers:** 7 in.
Each "layer" will hold one battery tray comprised of 4 of 8 cells
Racks come with lockable castor wheels
##  Useful Tips for Planning Your Battery Testing Setup
How will the device under test (cell, module, or pack) be connected to the Arbin system? _Collapse_
  * Individual cables
  * Individual cell holders (coin cells, cylindrical cells, flat/pouch cells)
  * Trays to hold multiple cells (coin cells, cylindrical cells, flay/pouch cells)
  * Racks to organize cells
  * Chambers

How close will the Arbin system be to where the devices are beign tested? _Expand_
  * Some cell holders will plug directly into the front of the Arbin tester
  * Cables may be used to connect directly to a battery. Cable length and connection type should be noted
  * Cables may be used to connect to a cell holder or rack. Cable length should be noted.

Will testing need to take place in a temperature chamber, safety chamber, or glovebox? _Expand_
  * Arbin offers an 8-zone temperature chamber for battery testing (**[MZTC](https://www.arbin.com/battery-research/multi-zone-temperature-chamber/)**) with an operating temperature of 10-60C and for cells rated up to 60A. Connections are made easy with the cables between the chamber and tester.
  * Cables may run from the Arbin tester into a glovebox or other temperature chamber. Cable length, temperature rating, and connection type should be noted.
