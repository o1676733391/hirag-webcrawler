#  10th International Conference on Sodium Batteries (ICNaB) 2025 in Sydney
October 1, 2025
![10th international conference on sodium batteries icnab 2025 in sydney](https://www.arbin.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/10th-international-conference-on-sodium-batteries-icnab-2025-in-sydney.png)
We are excited to announce our participation as a sponsor at the **10th International Conference on Sodium Batteries ( ICNaB) 2025**, hosted by the **University of Technology Sydney**. The event location is **Amora Hotel Jamison Sydney, 11 Jamison Street, Sydney, NSW 22000.**
This prestigious event serves as a vital platform for leading academics, emerging researchers, and industry pioneers to exchange ideas, present cutting-edge research, and foster meaningful collaboration in the field of **sodium-ion battery technology.**
The topics that the event will cover:
  * Sodium-ion batteries
  * Sodium-sulfur batteries
  * Sodium-air (oxygen) batteries
  * Electrode materials
  * Electrolytes
  * Binder and separator
  * Battery system design

The event proudly welcomes some of the most renowned professors and researchers in the field of sodium battery technology, including Professor Shinichi Komaba from Tokyo University and Professor Amartya Mukhopadhyay from the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay.

As global demand grows for **sustainable, cost-effective, and scalable** energy storage solutions, sodium-ion batteries are emerging as a compelling alternative to lithium-based systems. The ICNaB conference will highlight breakthroughs in materials science, electrochemical performance, battery architecture, and commercialization strategies—driving us closer to a cleaner energy future.
At **Arbin Instruments** , we are proud to support this important initiative. With nearly **35 years of experience** in high-precision battery testing systems, we remain committed to empowering innovation across the battery industry.
We invite you to visit us at the conference and discover our **latest technological advancements** designed to support next-generation battery research and development.
The event is from Oct 6, 2025 to Oct 10, 2025, 9am to 5pm. For more info, please visit [ICNaB2025 Home](https://utscleanenergy.org/icnab-2025/)
We look forward to seeing you in **Sydney**!
