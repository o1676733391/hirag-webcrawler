# Overview

Cold can be a threat to car batteries. Drivers in below-freezing temperatures routinely must deal with dead batteries that need a jump. Researchers at Penn State University developed a solution to this problem with a new battery that has an extra material layer enabling cold charging.

# Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Self-Assembled Monolayer | A one molecule layer thick piece of material that bonds to a surface due to chemical forces during a deposition process. |
| Surface Reaction Element | Protects the lithium anode and prevents dendrite formation during charging. |
| On-Demand Protection | The decomposed part of the original layer reforms on top of the lithium, preventing dendrite formation. |
