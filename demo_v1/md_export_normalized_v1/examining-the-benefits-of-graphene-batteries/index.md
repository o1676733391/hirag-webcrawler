# Overview

Graphene batteries leverage their namesake material, which sees carbon atoms bind together in a honeycomb lattice pattern to form an extremely strong, yet thin sheet. Graphene has a long list of beneficial properties – it conducts electrical and thermal energy extremely well, is lightweight and flexible, and is chemically inert.

# Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Surface area improvement | Graphene improves a battery’s surface area, leading to better power and energy density. |
| Enhanced conductivity | Graphene boosts conductivity without elevated temperatures, optimizing energy transfer and performance. |
| Longer-lasting batteries | Graphene allows batteries to last for more charge cycles and charge more quickly. |
