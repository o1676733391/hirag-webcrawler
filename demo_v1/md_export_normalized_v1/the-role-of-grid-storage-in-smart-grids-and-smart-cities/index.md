# Overview

Arbin products deliver fast, accurate self-discharge current measurement while maintaining battery electrochemical balance. The technology allows the applied small current to be constant and unaffected by temperature changes, ensuring precision throughout the process.

# Notes

- Autocalibration box is sold separately.
- EIS testing device is sold separately.
- What affects test equipment precision includes resolution of DAC, resolution of ADC, non-linearity of calibration, short-term drift (temperature), and long-term drift (material characteristics).
- Arbin test equipment improvements include higher resolution, improved software algorithms, new methods of thermal management, new patented shunt design, new method of time keeping, and new materials.
