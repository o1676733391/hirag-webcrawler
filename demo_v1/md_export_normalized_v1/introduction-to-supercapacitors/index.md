# Overview

Supercapacitors are energy storage devices that store electricity as static rather than chemical energy. They have advantages over batteries in terms of power density and charging speed, making them suitable for high power applications.

# Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Energy density | 1 to 30 Wh/kg |
| Power density | Up to 10,000 W/kg |
| Charging time | 1-10 seconds |
| Cycle life | Longer than batteries |
