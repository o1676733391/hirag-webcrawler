# Overview

Battery technology is ever evolving as research and development into different materials, configurations, and designs bring new insights and breakthroughs. As the world moves towards green energy, batteries play an important role in powering the future.

# Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Maturing solid-state batteries | Solid-state batteries replace flammable liquid electrolytes with solid alternatives, mitigating fire risks. |
| Non-lithium batteries | Research into batteries that do not use lithium, such as CO2 batteries for grid-scale storage. |
| Arbin support | Arbin provides high-quality battery test equipment to support research and development. |
