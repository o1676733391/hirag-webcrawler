# Overview

Magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) technology is being utilized to diagnose and optimize lithium-ion and sodium batteries, allowing researchers to observe internal processes without destroying the batteries.

# Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Lithium-Ion Diagnosis | MRI technology measures alterations in magnetic fields to analyze battery performance. |
| Sodium Monitoring | MRI detects movements of sodium metal ions in sodium batteries, enabling observation of internal processes. |
